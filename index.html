Hi!

Tim Berners-Lee is a software engineer who invented the Internet (or the World Wide Web) in 1989 while at CERN, the European Particle Physics Laboratory. He is director of the World Wide Web Consortium that he founded and a Professor in the Computer Science Department at the University of Oxford. Founded: World Wide Web Consortium Parents: Mary Lee Woods, Conway Berners-Lee Born: June 8, 1955, London

Sir Timothy John Berners-Lee OM KBE FRS FREng FRSA FBCS, also known as TimBL, is an English computer scientist best known as the inventor of the World Wide Web. He is a Professorial Fellow of Computer Science at the University of Oxford and a professor at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology. Wikipedia Born: June 8, 1955 (age 65 years), London, United Kingdom Spouse: Rosemary Leith (m. 2014), Nancy Carlson (m. 1990–2011) Education: The Queen's College (1973–1976), Emanuel School (1969–1973), Sheen Mount Primary School Awards: MacArthur Fellowship, Turing Award, MORE Children: Ben Berners-Lee, Alice Berners-Lee Quotes You affect the world by what you browse.
